The Writer's Nook - Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: The Writer's Nook

Reason: The name reflects the focus on offering beginner-friendly writing tips and book recommendations.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help aspiring writers with tips and provide book recommendations to readers, helping them discover new stories to enjoy.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What are some simple writing tips to help me get started on my first story?

Scenario 2: Which books would you recommend for someone who enjoys mystery novels?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #4A90E2 (Blue) - Used for headings, buttons, and accent elements.

Secondary Color: #F5A623 (Orange) - Used for background accents and important highlights.

Typography

Heading Font: 'Merriweather', serif - Used for headings.

Body Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - Used for regular text and paragraphs.
